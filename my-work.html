Andy Warhol: A Case Study

An in-depth examination of Andy Warhol's artistic career through systematic review and analysis. The study explores five distinct periods:

  1. Exploration of images (1928-1949)
  2. Commercial images (1949-1962)
  3. Pop images (1962-1968)
  4. Experimental films (1963-1968)
  5. Commercial films and return to images (1968-1987)
